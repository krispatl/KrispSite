Commercial Collaborations

I bring speculative storytelling and emerging technology into the commercial sphere—blending immersive design, cinematic environments, and interactive systems for clients ranging from global brands to record labels and publishers.

Migos Hologram Activation – Proto Hologram

In collaboration with Proto Inc., I helped bring Migos to life as full-scale holograms in multiple cities simultaneously—an ambitious, high-tech campaign that redefined presence and music marketing. The activation appeared in Atlanta, Las Vegas, LA, and Miami.

Under Armour – Coaches Armoury

Built an experiential learning platform using Unreal Engine and pixel streaming for Under Armour’s training ecosystem. Athletes could engage with immersive, interactive content tailored to their journey.

Random House Books – Augmented Reality Narrative

Created an AR companion experience for the novel Home Is Not a Country by Safia Elhillo. Using volumetric capture and interactive storytelling, we extended the world of the book into spatial media.

"Pardon" Music Video

Featuring T.I. and Lil Baby. I designed projection-mapped environments and custom visuals to expand the cinematic narrative.

Bose AR Adventure

Developed a spatial audio game layer for Bose audio wearables, turning real-world movement into an immersive story.

NIKE House of Hoops – NBA All-Star Weekend

Designed and installed synchronized projection and video systems for NIKE’s experiential takeover in Charlotte.

Projection Mapping for Hotel Indigo

Activated the George Washington Masonic Memorial with digital architecture and immersive visuals.

“Boy Erased” (Set Artwork)

My art was featured on the set of Boy Erased, distributed on HBO and Amazon Prime.

Trinidad James – Album Cover

Visual direction for the cover art of Trinidad James’ “All Lives Matter” release.

ABC Studios – “Kevin Probably Saves The World”

Created motion graphics and 3D modeling for network broadcast.

Live Visuals – MADEINTYO + BJ The Chicago Kid

Audio-reactive live visuals for a Fader Magazine + Redd’s Cider release event.

Herradura – Tequila Barrel Installation

Commissioned to reimagine a tequila barrel as an interactive sculptural piece blending mythology and industrial design.
