Environments That Breathe

I build portals—some shimmer in public parks, others glow behind museum walls. My work in installation art and projection mapping is a practice of spatial storytelling: conjuring speculative architectures, translating data into emotion, and letting light inhabit material like spirit in a vessel.

Below are fragments of that journey—rituals of presence, encoded transmissions, temporal anomalies...

Selected Works

Liquid Light

A 30-meter projection-mapped waterfall cascading down the Woodruff Park water wall. Commissioned by Dashboard and powered by networked 40K lumen projectors, this piece became a living monument to perseverance.
