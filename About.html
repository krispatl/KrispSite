Kris Pilcher

Kris Pilcher is an award winning installation and mixed reality artist based in Atlanta, GA.

Kris’ work has been exhibited nationally and internationally in gallery, entertainment, and public art settings. His work has been featured in numerous publications and exhibits, including:

The Sheffield International Doc/Fest, Elevate Atlanta, Forecast Public Art Review, The World Youth Forum in Geneva, Switzerland, Google VR, and Netflix.

clients include: ABC Studios, Fader Magazine, and the Will and Jada Pinkett Smith Foundation.

" I am interested in how altered existences are currently being created through our rapidly advancing technology

and how these new realities affect our cognition and interaction with the normality of physical existence.

I believe that mankind is on a precipice of technological evolution and I like living in the future"
