DIGITAL DIVIDE

An exhibition of work inspired by the technology, aesthetic, and society of the digital age.

The Internet and other forms of communication sharing are transforming society, improving our mutual understanding, eliminating power differentials, and helping to realize a truly free and democratic world society. This exhibition will explore the implications and influence of our digital society through work from national and internationally acclaimed visual and performance artists.
