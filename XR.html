Extended Reality Projects

My XR work spans immersive art, interactive storytelling, and AI-driven mixed reality systems—blurring lines between the real and virtual across headsets, mobile platforms, and public installations.

Common-AI-Verse – CCCB

A landmark exhibition at the Centre de Cultura Contemporània de Barcelona (CCCB), commissioned by CCCB and produced by Espronceda Institute of Art & Culture. As installation advisor, I collaborated with digital artists and AI researchers to shape a large-scale, co-creative metaverse space powered by emotion-recognizing AI. Visitors interacted with laser lights, reactive sound, and mirrored interfaces to co-create evolving digital objects with AI agents.

Writer’s Block – Mixed Reality Hip Hop Tribute

Writer’s Block draws inspiration from the vibrant streets of NYC, the iconic birthplace of hip hop culture. This mixed reality experience transforms your space into a living tribute to the movement—where you embody a breakdancer or graffiti artist in real time. Legendary artwork by Lady Pink and beats by Madlib infuse the experience with soul.

Built using the Movement SDK with real-time body pose capture, hand tracking, gesture recognition, passthrough, spatial audio, interactables, and room mesh. Users can pause their avatar mid-dance to study poses from any angle.

Created in collaboration with Daniel Trujillo and Nick Kaufmann.
