Dream Collection Agency

The Dream Collection Agency is a long-form speculative installation and transmedia research project exploring the boundaries between reality, memory, and the subconscious. Originally conceived as a performative sci-fi corporation, the DCA collects, preserves, and reactivates human dreams using a combination of analog ritual, volumetric capture, and XR storytelling systems.

Housed in custom-built Dream Experience Facilities®, the agency guides participants through curated dream recall sessions which are then encoded into visual form. These sessions use multisensory cues, projection mapping, and biometric feedback to access deep memory states and blurred liminal moments.

The project asks: what happens when dreams are archived like data? Can we preserve the subconscious? And if so, who owns the past that never happened?
